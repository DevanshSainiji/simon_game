Instructions:

This is a memory game. First the computer will click a button (maybe green, red, yellow, blue) and then you have to click that particular button along with all the previously pressed buttons.

Example:- Lets say the game starts and the computer pressed green button and then you have to press green button and after that you will reach level 2 and after that the computer pressed blue button then you have to press green button and then blue button.

And like this the game goes on and on.
